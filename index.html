about me

Hello! My name is Myumi and I like to write code. Descended from Japanese royalty and a childhood addiction to Neopets, I started with the basics programming at an early age. An in-game CSS guide gave me my first dip into code, however, it wasn't until high school that I realized HTML/CSS/JS wasn't just for Neopets.

Since then, I have travelled across the country to work as a video game developer, and flown back home to New Jersey pursue software development. Now, I have begun to root myself into what made me the happiest when I was programming: web application development and design.

I can most frequently be found sewing new toys for my eccentric and elderly furrball, cooking up something vegan and delicious, or playing nostalgic video game melodies on bass.

Whether you're interested in collborating on something fun or inquiring about an interview, I can best be reached via email.
