Hello! My name is Myumi and I like to write code. Descended from Japanese royalty and a childhood addiction to Neopets, I started the basics programming at an early age. Since then, I have travelled across the country to work as a video game developer, and flown back home to New Jersey pursue software development. Now, I am looking to root myself back into what made me the happiest when I was programming: web design and development.

I can most frequently be found sewing new toys for my eccentric and elderly furrball, cooking up something vegan and delicious, or playing nostalgic video game melodies on bass.
